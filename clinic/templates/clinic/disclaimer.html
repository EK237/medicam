COVID-19 Doctor

Please read and acknowledge the following:

  • This website is provided in good faith as a last resort for those who are otherwise unable to obtain help and advice during this unprecedented public health emergency.
  • This website is not a healthcare provider, and it does not connect you to licensed healthcare providers. If you have a medical emergency, please call a doctor or go to the emergency room.

  • Your name or other personally identifying information will not be stored. Your IP address will be stored to help protect our systems.
